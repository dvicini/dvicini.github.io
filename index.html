About

I am a senior research scientist at Google Zurich, where I work on inverse rendering, digital humans and optical simulation. Before joining Google, I completed my PhD at EPFL's Realistic Graphics Lab, working under the supervision of Wenzel Jakob. My PhD thesis is on efficient and accurate physically-based differentiable rendering algorithms.

I obtained my bachelor at the University of Bern and my master from ETH Zurich, where I completed my master thesis at Disney Research. I worked as an intern for Facebook Reality Labs, Walt Disney Animation Studios and Disney Research.
