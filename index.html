About

I am a fifth year PhD student at the Realistic Graphics Lab, working under the supervision of Wenzel Jakob. Before joining EPFL, I obtained my bachelor at the University of Bern and completed my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Matthias Zwicker and Marco Manzi. After that, I moved to ETH Zurich for my master. I did my master thesis at Disney Research, working with Jan Novák and Fabrice Rousselle. I worked as an intern for Disney Research, Walt Disney Animation Studios and Facebook Reality Labs. My main research interests are efficient physically-based and differentiable rendering. I am scheduled to graduate September 2022.
